Safe and Secure Shopping




Is it safe to use my credit/debit card on Get It Shopping?

Your online transaction on Get It Shopping is secure with the highest levels of transaction security currently available on the Internet. Get It Shopping uses 256-bit encryption technology to protect your card information while securely transmitting it to the respective banks for payment processing. All credit card and debit card payments on Get It Shopping are processed through secure and trusted payment gateways managed by leading banks. Banks now use the 3D Secure password service for online transactions, providing an additional layer of security through identity verification.




Does Get It Shopping store my credit/debit card infomation?

Get It Shooping stores the first 6 and last 4 digits of your card number in a secure and encrypted manner. The first 6 digits (also known as the Bank Identification Number) are used to identify the bank name and country where your card was issued. The first 6 and last 4 digits are together used for fraud detection and prevention purposes.

Yes! What other payment options are available on Get It Shopping?

Apart from Credit and Debit Cards, we accept payments by Internet Banking (covering 44 banks), Cash-on-Delivery, Equated Monthly Installments (EMI), E-Gift Vouchers, PhonePe UPI and PhonePe wallet.
